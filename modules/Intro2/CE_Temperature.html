Background

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) maintains a database of global land and sea temperatures. The data can be accessed from their Climate at a Glance website. Their default settings show the global temperature anomaly (difference from a long-term average) for February and are in this CSV file. The data file is loaded into R below,1 but note that skip=4 is need as the first four lines of the CSV file do not contain data.2

#!# Set to your own working directory and have just your filename below.
gt <- read.csv("https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/cag/global/time-series/globe/land_ocean/1/2/1880-2020/data.csv",skip=4)
