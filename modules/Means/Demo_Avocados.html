Background

Kaggle has a database of the price and sales of avocados from many areas around the United States from 2015-2018. The data is here (with metadata under “columns” at this site).1 Their data are loaded below2 and, for this exercise, reduced to only results for the “GreatLakes” region (i.e., filter()), and type and year are converted to groupings (i.e., factor() within mutate()).3

#!# Set to your own working directory and have just your filename below.
avoc <- read.csv("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/droglenc/NCData/master/Avocados.csv",
                stringsAsFactors=FALSE,quote="") %>%
  filter(region=="GreatLakes") %>%
  mutate(type=factor(type),
         year=factor(year))
str(avoc)
