Background

Kaggle has a database of the price and sales of avocados from many areas around the United States from 2015-2018. The data is here (with metadata under “columns” at this site.1 Before loading these data, load the tidyverse package, which will load ggplot2 along with a few other packages you will need to access these data.

library(tidyverse)

Their data are loaded below and, for this exercise, reduced to only results for the “GreatLakes” region and “conventional” (not “organic”) avocados. In addition, year was forced to be a factor variable.2

avoc <- read.csv("https://derekogle.com/NCGraphing/modules/zdata/avocados.csv",
                stringsAsFactors=FALSE) %>%
  filter(region=="GreatLakes",type=="conventional") %>%
  mutate(year=factor(year))
str(avoc)
